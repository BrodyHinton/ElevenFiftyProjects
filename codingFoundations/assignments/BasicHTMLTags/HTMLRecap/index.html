Drafting Guide

In the first few rounds your focus should be RBs and WRs since they give you the most points and they are the least replacable out of every position. In the early-mid rounds you can start focusing on your TEs and QBs, primarily QBs that can run well since rushing yards/tds give you more points than passing. In the last few rounds you can draft your kicker and defense since they are the most replacable. Also kickers are usually luck based on how many times their offense gives them the opportunity to kick, and defenses can be switched out on a week-by-week basis depending on how good the offense they're facing is.
